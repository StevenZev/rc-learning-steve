Numeric comparisons are possible:

-eq 	equal

-ne 	not equal

-gt 	greater than

-ge 	greater than or equal

-lt 	less than

-le	less than or equal

a=2

if [[ $a -eq 2 ]] ; then

echo It is two.

fi
